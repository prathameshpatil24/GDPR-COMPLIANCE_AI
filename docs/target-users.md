# Target users

The canonical **target users** document is **[Phase 0.3 – Target Users](phase-0-overview/03-target-users.md)** (version scope including **v4** is in section 4).

## Version 4 (planned) — headline

v4 prioritises **near-100% retrieval accuracy** through **deterministic article mapping** and **LLM-based verification**, so the system can be positioned for **real-world legal compliance** use with explicit limits. That is followed by a **retrieval gap tracker** for continuous improvement, **multilingual retrieval** for the German market, **document upload** for privacy-policy-style analysis, and **website scanning** for cookie and privacy compliance checks.

**What to expect in outputs (v4):** Structured **confidence** on claims and **explicit uncertainty** when the system cannot ground an answer — preferable to confident wrong answers in a legal domain.
